# Books

The inputs that shape this publication's lens. Not a reading list — a declaration of what has been absorbed and why it matters to our reading of the world.

## Foundational (re-read)

| Book | Author | Why it matters |
|------|--------|----------------|
| The 48 Laws of Power | Robert Greene | The grammar of political behavior. Named the patterns so they could be seen. |
| The 50th Law | Robert Greene & 50 Cent | The psychological core beneath the 48: fearlessness as the precondition of sight. |
| The Art of War | Sun Tzu | The strategist's mind, compressed. Position, deception, timing. |
| Poor Charlie's Almanac | Charlie Munger | The latticework of mental models. Inversion, incentives, Lollapalooza. |
| The Analects | Confucius | The gentleman's conduct. Long-horizon virtue as competitive advantage. |

## Rule for adding a book

A book earns a place here only if it changed a frame. A pleasant read alone does not qualify. When adding: one line on what it made you see that you could not see before.
